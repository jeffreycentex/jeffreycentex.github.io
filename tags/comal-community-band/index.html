Comal Community Band

The Comal Community Band is a local community band in New Braunfels. We rehearse most Monday nights from 6:45 PM - 8:30 PM at the New Braunfels ISD Ninth Grade Center located on Guenther Avenue (we take a hiatus after the fourth of July until school restarts and also have a winter break that corresponds with the Christmas holidays for NBISD)
